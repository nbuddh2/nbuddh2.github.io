Percentage of the US populace by age demographic


This is a graphical analytics demonstration that depicts the age hierarchy in the United States and demonstrates how age distribution change over time. It was created with animated graphics by Pew Research and is simple to distribute on social media or embed in blogs. They employ a distribution to depict the changes in intellectual coherence as a function of the occurrence of factors within a set of data.


They also employ bar graphs to demonstrate how the demographic structure varies over history and to enable the viewer to see numerous age groups in one go, with distinct colors for men and women, and various layouts to show: proportion of U.S. populace by age range, 1950-2060; regions with the greatest number of homicides per 100,000 people; average intellectual ranking on a 10-point scale of intellectual coherence of those who obtained information from each channel within a week; Proportion of endorsing same-sex marriage
