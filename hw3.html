Point Cloud and Scatter Plot

    Operations :

    Pressing the 'up','down','left and right' keys, move the cylinder.

    Utilizing the cursor, rotate and zoom the point cloud cylinder.

    To show crossing points with a point cloud on a scatter plot, move the plane with the 'a' (right) and 'd' (left) keys.

    Brushing on the scatter plot will be seen by drawing a rectangle around the spots you want to choose.
